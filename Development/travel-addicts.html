CLIENT - AIRBALTIC

Everything is better with cherries. Cake gets a sense, champagne becomes drinkable and girls get naughty. Everyone who travels with airBaltic already knows that tickets with cherries are better – they mean discounts for flights and probably a champagne 11 kilometers up in the air. We brought those small, hard- to-find, but widely loved cherries to life, gave them a tiny dose of common sense and a large portion of humor – this way we created series of videos for airBaltic where cherries are waiting for the right moment to go on juicy adventures.
IDEA 3D ANIMATION SOUND
As always, cherry-life was brought up by design and animation, common sense was put in by our tailor made strategy, but humor... Humor came fast and as a surprise – almost like airBaltic’s cherry deals themselves. In the end Latvia’s airline got active and attractive videos that can be communicated whatever one’s heart feels. But we? We got a chance to work with the most punctual airline in the World, twitch cherries by their stems and dream about 50 different destinations for our next vacation. Eee!
