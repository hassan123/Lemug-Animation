THE CLOUD IS COMING

CLIENT - ATEA GLOBAL SERVICES

“What cloud services are?” asked one entrepreneur to another. “Dunno, some NASA stuff I guess!” That's how many entrepreneurs have been talking about a groundbreaking technology that has been around for some time. Is that normal? Nope. “But what to do about it?” guys from ATEA asked when we met for tea and cookies. “Let's design and animate!” we said and rushed to our desks ending up with five simple and clear videos about clouds, the possibility of storing and securing data on them as well as how to use them by making any entrepreneurs' day easier.

IDEA DESIGN ANIMATION SOUND DESIGN

We created three teasers, one explanatory and one ad video with a simple goal – to show how two different entrepreneurs act in sudden situations. How? Very differently. Not always a guy who takes a life-vest with him to the beach is a winner at the end of the day. But that guy who invests in new technologies will win not just at the end of the day but on the next day's brunch too. 

“Why so many videos?” asked the guys from ATEA when we met for our presentation and comments. “So it's more clear!” we answered and started to render final_video.mov. However, if taking this seriously - a topic that has been spoken by so many, but is understood by just a few, had to be brought up in a new and easy-to-get lighting. From now on “cloud services” won't be a dark and cloudy word – it's going to be a clear and sunny topic to any entrepreneur.
